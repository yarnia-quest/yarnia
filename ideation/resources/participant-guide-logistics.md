## Pitch deck: max 7 slides

Every slide must do work:

1. **Problem + customer** — specific pain, who has it, what they do today to solve it
2. **Solution + product** — what you built, how it works, demo link/QR if available
3. **Why now** — what changed that makes this possible (new API, regulation, cost collapse)
4. **Market + competition** — bottom-up sizing, direct competitors, why you win (never say "we have no competitors")
5. **Business model + traction/evidence** — who pays, how much, how often, what you validated
6. **Go-to-market** — how the first 50 customers find you (name a real channel)
7. **Team** — why this team has the right edge. End with contact info.

## Rules
