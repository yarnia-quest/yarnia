# 2025-02-24 21:06 - Finn, Ella und das Geheimnis im Glas

## Session Context

Story time: 2025-02-24 21:06 Europe/Berlin.

Another found-object story. The forest contains traces of old memories.

## Story Summary

Finn and Ella are walking through the forest when something shines between the leaves. Finn pulls an old window pane from the ground.

In the glass, they notice a small heart scratched into the surface. Finn calls it a secret heart and wonders who the pane belongs to.

They ask animals in the forest. The badger says he lives underground and does not need windows. The fox remembers seeing an old window once but does not claim it. Others are unsure.

Then an excited squirrel recognizes the window pane and reacts as if it belongs to a known story.

## Continuity Value

- Continues the motif of old objects carrying memories.
- Adds the scratched heart as a gentle mystery symbol.
- Supports Yarnia's memory layer: old details can return later.
