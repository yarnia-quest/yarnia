# 2025-01-27 21:55 - Finn, Ella und der verpasste Theaterbesuch

## Session Context

Story time: 2025-01-27 21:55 Europe/Berlin.

A disappointment-repair story. Finn misses something he wanted, and Ella helps create a softer alternative.

## Story Summary

Finn is excited to go to a theater performance with Ella, but he oversleeps. When Ella taps at his window, the sun is already high, and Finn realizes they have missed it.

Finn is upset. Ella gently suggests they can still make the day special. She leads Finn into the snowy forest.

Together they build a tiny theater from branches, pine cones, and forest materials. Ella tells stories from a branch stage, and Finn becomes the audience.

The missed theater visit turns into their own forest theater day.

## Continuity Value

- Shows Ella's emotional role: she helps Finn transform disappointment.
- Establishes imagination as a central problem-solving tool.
- Adds the snowy forest theater as a memory Yarnia can reference later.
