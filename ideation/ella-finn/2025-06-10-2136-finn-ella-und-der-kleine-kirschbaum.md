## Story Summary

Spring has come. The meadows are green, bees are humming, and small flowers are everywhere.

Ella suggests planting a tree. Finn is excited and asks what kind. Ella chooses a cherry tree because it blossoms beautifully and later gives sweet cherries.

They ask the old turtle by the pond where to find a cherry tree. The turtle points them toward a hill behind the lilac bush. There, they find cherries and save a cherry pit.

Back at the forest house, Finn digs a hole, and Ella drops the pit inside. They cover it with soil, water it, and sit beside it.

Ella explains that it will not grow immediately. It needs sun, rain, patience, and love. Finn imagines future animals sitting in its shade and eating cherries.
