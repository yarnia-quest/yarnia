# 2025-06-03 22:07 - Ella, der Bilderrahmen und der Schwan

## Session Context

Story time: 2025-06-03 22:07 Europe/Berlin.

A repair-and-kindness story centered on Ella making a mistake.

## Story Summary

On a quiet afternoon, Ella flies through the forest house while Finn plays outside. Sunlight catches a picture frame on the wall.

The picture shows a white swan on a calm lake. Ella has never seen a swan or a picture frame like this before. Curious, she touches it gently with her wing.

The frame falls and breaks. Ella is upset and apologizes when Finn comes inside.

Finn sees that the picture itself is still safe. Instead of blaming Ella, he suggests they build a new frame. They collect bark, twigs, moss, resin, and spiderwebs, then make a new forest frame together.

## Continuity Value

- Shows repair instead of blame.
- Gives Ella a vulnerable moment.
- Adds the swan picture and handmade frame as recurring home details.
