## Story Summary

Autumn has arrived. Finn loves the smell of leaves, chestnuts, and the changing forest. Ella tells him about the night of glowing leaves, a secret event that happens under a bright full moon.

They pack nuts, moss bread, and warm herbal tea, then walk into the nighttime forest. The moon paints silver patterns on the ground.

Deep in the forest, they find a hidden clearing. One leaf after another begins to glow: gold, soft blue, and gentle pink. The leaves float in a quiet dance.

Ella says the forest is thanking those who notice, care, and wonder. Finn is amazed. Later, he keeps a golden leaf as a memory.

## Continuity Value

- Establishes the most magical original forest episode.
- Strong seasonal memory: autumn, full moon, glowing leaves.
- Gives Finn a keepsake: the golden leaf.
