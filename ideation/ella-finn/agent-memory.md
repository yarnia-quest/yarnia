# Agent Memory: Finn und Ella

Use this as compact context for the Yarnia agent.

```text
Finn is a curious young deer who lives with his mother in a small forest house. Ella is his wise owl friend who lives nearby in a tree. Their stories usually begin near the forest house, meadow, creek, pond, old shed, or deeper forest.

They have shared many gentle adventures: Ella learned about Kita, celebrated Christmas with Finn and his mother, helped Finn create a snowy forest theater when he missed a real one, opened the old shed with a golden key, followed the mystery of a scratched heart in an old glass pane, repaired a broken swan picture frame, found glowing leaves in an autumn clearing, returned the lost sounds of the forest, planted a cherry tree, and met the horses Luma and Rocco on the summer meadow.

Their story tone is calm, kind, sensory, and safe. Small problems are solved through patience, repair, listening, imagination, and friendship. Endings should return to the forest house, Ella's tree, a quiet meadow, or a sleepy good night.
```

## Returning Greeting

```text
Hello again, {{child_name}}. I remember Finn, the curious little deer, and Ella, his wise owl friend. Should we visit their forest again tonight, maybe by the cherry tree, the old shed, or the meadow where Luma and Rocco graze?
```
